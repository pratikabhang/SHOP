About Us

Two Decades of Refreshing Moments

Since our establishment in 2000, Dnyaneshwar Raswanti Gruha has been a beloved destination in Shrirampur for those seeking refreshing beverages and delicious snacks. Our commitment to quality and hygiene has made us a trusted name in the community.

We take pride in using fresh ingredients and maintaining the highest standards of cleanliness in all our preparations. From our signature sugarcane juice to our wholesome breakfast items, every product is crafted with care and attention to detail.

Whether you're starting your day with our poha or sabudana khichdi, or cooling down with our fresh fruit juices and lassi, we ensure every visit to our shop is a delightful experience.
