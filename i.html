Our Story Since 2000

Dnyaneshwar Raswanti Gruha has been proudly serving the people of Shrirampur since the year 2000. For over two decades, we've been the go-to destination for refreshing drinks and tasty treats.

We take pride in preparing all our beverages with the highest standards of hygiene and quality, using fresh ingredients to ensure authentic taste in every sip.

Whether you're looking to cool down with a chilled drink or enjoy a quick snack, Dnyaneshwar Raswanti Gruha is the ideal place to relax and refresh with our friendly service.
